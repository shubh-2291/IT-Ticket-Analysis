## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of IT service desk operations based on **97,498 tickets** handled by **50 agents** over a **5-year period (2016–2020)**.

The objective is to uncover patterns in ticket resolution, agent performance, and customer satisfaction, and to provide **data-driven recommendations** for improving IT support efficiency.

## 📊 Key Insights

### 📈 Ticket Trends
- Ticket volume steadily increased from 2016 to 2020  
- Sharp rise in 2020 indicates growing IT dependency  

---

### ⏱️ Resolution Time & Satisfaction
- Avg resolution time: ~4.5 days (stable)  
- Satisfaction improved from 3.98 → 4.16  

**Insight:** Efficient scaling without quality drop  

---

### ⚙️ Request Category Analysis
- Hardware: Highest resolution time (~7.6 days)  
- Login Access: Fastest (~0.31 days)  

**Insight:** Bottlenecks exist in hardware & system-related issues  

---

### 🚨 Severity vs Resolution Time
- Urgent tickets: Fastest resolution (~2 days)  
- Normal tickets: Slowest (~4.66 days)  

**Insight:** Priority handling is effective, but complex issues need improvement  

---

### 😊 Customer Satisfaction
- Majority ratings are 4 & 5  
- Very few low ratings  

**Insight:** Strong service quality  

---

### 👨‍💼 Agent Performance
- Workload is evenly distributed  
- Performance gaps are moderate  

**Insight:** Training is more important than hiring

## 📉 Correlation Insight

- Correlation between Severity & Resolution Time = -0.0405  

**Insight:** Very weak relationship → complexity matters more than severity

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

### ✅ Focus on Training (Highest ROI)
- Improve handling of complex tickets  
- Enhance technical and communication skills  

---

### 🤖 Automate Repetitive Tasks
- Chatbots for FAQs  
- Auto ticket classification  
- Workflow optimization  

---

### 💻 Upgrade Technology (Secondary Priority)
- Better ticketing tools  
- Intelligent routing  
- Knowledge base integration  

---

### 📅 Plan for Peak Demand
- Q4 = highest ticket volume  
- Use Q1/Q2 for training and upgrades  

---

### 👨‍💼 Agent Development Strategy
- Identify low-performing agents  
- Provide targeted training  
- Monitor performance  

---

## 📌 Key Conclusions

- IT support system is stable and scalable  
- Increasing demand is handled efficiently  
- Resolution efficiency depends on complexity, not urgency  
- Training > Hiring for performance improvement  
- Future gains depend on:
  - Skill development  
  - Process optimization  
  - Automation
